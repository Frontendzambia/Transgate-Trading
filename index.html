About Our Company

Founded in 2022, Transgate Trading has grown from a single-truck operation into a leading logistics provider in Zambia. Our journey is built on a foundation of integrity, punctuality, and an unwavering commitment to our clients.

Our values—Reliability, Safety, and Innovation—guide every decision we make. We leverage cutting-edge technology and a team of dedicated professionals to ensure your cargo is handled with the utmost care, from pickup to delivery. With years of experience, we understand the nuances of the Zambian market and are prepared to overcome any logistical challenge.
